Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a free collection service acroos the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this

removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Useing us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the
waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion all the
equipment we collect.
